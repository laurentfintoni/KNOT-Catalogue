Which universities are associated with research projects?

This map shows how a majority of projects are associated with universities based in the north of Italy, and in particular institutions with strong humanities departments. Each dot corresponds to a university and the number to the projects it is associated with.

What types of digital scholarly objects are being produced by humanities research in Italy?

Software and 'generic' datasets, most often RDF datasets, are the most prominent.

What types of services are being offered for scholarly digital objects?

The most common types of services are database and digital editions, reflecting the importance of philology within Italian humanities. Digital versions of archives, catalogues, and libraries are also well represented alongside digital platform which offers flexibility in how users can interact with objects.
